d4rkAvatarOptimizer is a Unity Editor tool by d4rkpl4y3r that boosts VRChat avatar performance by merging meshes, materials, and shader properties automatically at upload time.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install via VCC">
    Add the custom repository `https://d4rkc0d3r.github.io/vpm-repos/main.json` to VRChat Creator Companion, then add d4rkAvatarOptimizer to your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the component">
    In Unity, add the **d4rk Avatar Optimizer** component to your avatar's root GameObject (or any ancestor).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a preset">
    Select **Basic** for safe optimizations, **Shader Toggles** for maximum draw call reduction with supported shaders, or **Full** for the most aggressive settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload or preview">
    Click **Upload** — the optimizer runs automatically before upload. Or click **Create Optimized Copy** to preview results in the editor first.
  </Step>
</Steps>
